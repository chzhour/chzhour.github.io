About Me

I am currently a Research Software Engineer at Google Research. I received my Ph.D degree at Department of Computer Science and Engineering from Hong Kong University of Science and Technology in 2019, supervised by Prof. Nevin L. Zhang. Before that, I received my B.E. degree from Sun Yat-sen University in 2014 under the supervision of Prof. Liang Lin.

Research Interest

  • Computer Vision, Natural Language Processing
  • Neural Network Structure Learning and Compression, Model Interpretability
